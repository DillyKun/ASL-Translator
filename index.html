Signalize is an American Sign Language (ASL) translator that utilizes computer vision and machine learning as its core technologies. Our goal is to bridge the gap in understanding sign language and provide an educational tool for learning.

Try it Yourself

  1. Hold up a hand sign to the camera of your device
  2. The hand sign's translation will appear above the video feed

Train it Yourself

  1. Think of a hand sign you would like to train the model with
  2. Type the sign's translation into the "Input Label" section
  3. Hold your hand sign up into the camera
  4. Repeatedly press the "Train Model" button while your sign is in the camera's view
  5. Press the "Save Training" button once you are done
